About First State Inc.

P.J. Bale, president and founder of First State Inc., has been serving homeowners with complete attention to detail and an unwavering quest for quality since he first began business as PJ’s Lawn Service in 1978. Today, while PJ’s horticultural talents extend solely to the communities the company develops, First State Inc. is committed to a level of craftsmanship that is unparalleled in the industry. Whether it’s full-home renovation or new-home construction, we’ll make your home the home you’ve always wanted.
